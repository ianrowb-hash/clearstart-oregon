Petition sent to . Follow these steps to file it.

1
Print 3 copies
Print on standard 8.5×11 paper. Sign and date each copy in ink.
2
File at your County Circuit Court
Bring all 3 copies to the circuit court clerk's office. Filing fee is $281 (fee waiver available under ORS 21.682 if income-eligible).
3
DA is automatically notified
The court clerk serves the District Attorney. You'll receive a hearing date within 30–60 days.
4
Attend your hearing
Most expungement hearings are brief and uncontested. Bring your ID and one copy of your petition.
5
Record is sealed
After the judge signs the Order, LEDS (Oregon Law Enforcement Data System) is notified. Full propagation takes 30–90 days.
